Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Eimazing Studio ("the App") is a personal automation tool operated by its owner to manage the owner's own YouTube channel and content. This policy explains what data the App handles.

Who this applies to

The App is used solely by its owner to access the owner's own Google/YouTube account. It is not offered to the public and does not create accounts for other users.

What the App accesses

With the owner's explicit consent through Google OAuth, the App uses the YouTube Data API to:

  • upload videos to the owner's YouTube channel; and
  • read basic channel information needed to perform those uploads (youtube.upload and youtube.readonly scopes).

How data is stored and used

The OAuth token granted by Google is stored locally on the owner's own devices and is used only to authenticate uploads to the owner's channel. The App does not sell, share, or transfer this data to any third party, and does not use it for advertising or profiling.

Data retention and deletion

The OAuth token is retained only while the App is in use and can be revoked at any time by the owner at myaccount.google.com/permissions, which immediately ends the App's access.

Google API Services User Data Policy

The App's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
